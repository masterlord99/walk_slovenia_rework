=====================================================
   HOW TO UPDATE PHOTOS ON THE WALK SLOVENIA WEBSITE
=====================================================

This guide explains how to replace the placeholder (grey) images
with your own real photos. No coding needed — just rename and
replace files.


WHAT YOU NEED
-------------
- Your photos (any .jpg photos from your phone or camera work fine)
- A file manager (the "Files" app on your computer)


WHERE THE PHOTOS LIVE
---------------------
All photos are inside the "images" folder on your Desktop:

   Desktop > webpage > images

Inside "images" you will see folders organized exactly like the
website:

   images/
   ├── home/                        <-- Homepage background photos
   ├── easy-activities/             <-- Level 1 background photos
   │   ├── castles-historic-towns/  <-- Category background photos
   │   │   ├── predjama-castle/     <-- Trip photos
   │   │   ├── sneznik-castle/
   │   │   └── ...
   │   ├── city-life-and-culture/
   │   └── ...
   ├── active-experiences/          <-- Level 2
   └── challenging-adventures/      <-- Level 3


HOW THE FILES ARE NAMED
-----------------------
Every photo file has a descriptive name so you know what it is:

   predjama-castle-1.jpg    <-- First photo of Predjama Castle
   predjama-castle-2.jpg    <-- Second photo
   predjama-castle-3.jpg    <-- Third photo
   predjama-castle-4.jpg    <-- Fourth photo

Files with "hero" in the name are the big background images that
cycle at the top of each page:

   easy-activities-hero-1.jpg   <-- First cycling background
   easy-activities-hero-2.jpg   <-- Second cycling background
   (and so on...)


STEP-BY-STEP: HOW TO REPLACE A PHOTO
-------------------------------------

Example: You want to replace the first photo of Predjama Castle.

1. Find your real photo on your computer
   (e.g. "IMG_4523.jpg" from your phone)

2. Open the file manager and go to:
   Desktop > webpage > images > easy-activities >
   castles-historic-towns > predjama-castle

3. You will see these files:
   predjama-castle-1.jpg
   predjama-castle-2.jpg
   predjama-castle-3.jpg
   predjama-castle-4.jpg

4. DELETE the file you want to replace:
   Right-click "predjama-castle-1.jpg" > Delete

5. COPY your real photo into this folder:
   Copy "IMG_4523.jpg" and paste it here

6. RENAME your photo to match the old name:
   Right-click "IMG_4523.jpg" > Rename
   Type: predjama-castle-1.jpg
   Press Enter

7. Done! Open the website in your browser to see the change.
   (You may need to refresh the page with Ctrl+R or F5)


IMPORTANT RULES
---------------

Rule 1: THE FILENAME MUST STAY EXACTLY THE SAME
   The website looks for specific filenames. If the file is called
   "predjama-castle-1.jpg" your replacement must also be called
   "predjama-castle-1.jpg" — not "Predjama Castle 1.jpg" or
   "predjama-castle-1.JPG" or anything else.

Rule 2: THE FILE MUST BE A .jpg IMAGE
   If your photo is a .png or .heic, you need to convert it first.
   The easiest way: open it in any image viewer, click
   "Save As" or "Export", and choose JPEG/JPG format.

Rule 3: USE LANDSCAPE (WIDE) PHOTOS
   The website displays photos in wide format. Photos taken with
   your phone held sideways (landscape) will look best.
   Portrait (tall) photos will work but may get cropped.

Rule 4: BIGGER IS BETTER
   Use the largest version of your photos. The website will
   automatically scale them down. Small photos will look blurry.
   Ideal size: at least 1600 pixels wide.


QUICK REFERENCE: WHAT EACH FOLDER IS FOR
-----------------------------------------

images/home/
   -> The 5 big cycling background photos on the homepage
   -> These are the first thing visitors see

images/easy-activities/
   -> 5 background photos for the "Easy Activities" level page
   -> Plus subfolders for each category and trip

images/active-experiences/
   -> 5 background photos for the "Active Experiences" level page
   -> Plus subfolders for each category and trip

images/challenging-adventures/
   -> 5 background photos for the "Challenging Adventures" level page
   -> Plus subfolders for each category and trip

Inside each level folder, you will find CATEGORY folders:
   e.g. "castles-historic-towns", "city-life-and-culture", etc.
   -> Each has 5 hero background photos for that category page

Inside each category folder, you will find TRIP folders:
   e.g. "predjama-castle", "lake-bled", "mostnica-gorge", etc.
   -> Each has 4 photos for that specific trip


HOW MANY PHOTOS DO I NEED?
---------------------------
- Homepage:           5 photos  (home-hero-1.jpg to home-hero-5.jpg)
- Each level:         5 photos  (e.g. easy-activities-hero-1.jpg to 5)
- Each category:      5 photos  (e.g. castles-historic-towns-hero-1.jpg to 5)
- Each trip:          4 photos  (e.g. predjama-castle-1.jpg to 4)

Total: 333 photos to replace everything.

TIP: Start with the homepage photos (images/home/) and the
level hero photos — those have the biggest visual impact.
You can replace the rest over time.


TROUBLESHOOTING
---------------

"I replaced a photo but the website still shows the old one"
   -> Press Ctrl+R (or F5) to refresh the page
   -> If that doesn't work, press Ctrl+Shift+R to force refresh

"My photo looks stretched or cropped weird"
   -> Use a landscape (wide) photo instead of a portrait (tall) one

"The photo doesn't show up at all"
   -> Check the filename is exactly right (no spaces, no capitals
      where there shouldn't be, ends in .jpg not .JPG)
   -> Make sure the file is actually a JPEG image

"I want more than 4 photos for a trip"
   -> This requires editing the code (js/data.js). Ask your
      developer to add more image slots.

"I want fewer photos for a trip"
   -> Just leave the extra placeholder files as they are, or
      replace them all with your best photos. Having duplicates
      is fine.
